About Us

Budget friendly family-owned 2-star hotel in San Pedro with breathtaking views of the Port of Los Angeles. The best economical option in San Pedro and Rancho Palos Verdes. Our hotel is synonymous with a great travel value. There is nothing more important to our company than the safety and well-being of our guests, our team members, and the communities in which we operate. Palos Verdes Inn implement a variety of practices that help to prevent human trafficking through enhanced safety and security procedures; employee and franchise education, training and response; and partnerships and advocacy.

services

Housekeeping

We offer housekeeping services to all guests to maintain cleanliness, hygiene, and decor of your stay at the hotel. Limited once per stay.

Room Security

Safety and security are top priorities for our hotel. We offer copies of key to romms only to verified registered guests and their families.

Free Wifi

We offer free internet services to registered guests staying at the hotel. Please contact the front desk for details of the wifi network.

Pet-friendly

We welcome all guests along with thier pets. We charge a separate pet fee on a daily basis. Pets are not allowed to litter in the lobby.

Accesible

Our hotel is completely accessible with Low-height counters and sink, raised toilet seat, grab bar near toilet, and wheelchair-width doorways.

Air-Conditioned

Our hotel offers Air-Conditioned rooms with a built-in heater. Guests can feel comfortable by adjusting the temperature to thier convenience.
